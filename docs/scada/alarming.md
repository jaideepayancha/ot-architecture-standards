# Alarm Standards

## Philosophy

- Alarms must be actionable
- No nuisance alarms

## Priorities

| Priority | Use |
|--------|--------|
| High | Safety or production stop |
| Medium | Quality or downtime risk |
| Low | Advisory |

## Required Alarm Properties

- Name
- Priority
- Setpoint
- Clear condition
- Documentation

## Shelving

- Auto-shelving prohibited
- Operator-controlled only

## KPIs

- Alarm rate < 1 per 10 minutes
- Standing alarms < 5
